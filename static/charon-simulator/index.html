Rate Limiting Tiers

Limit: The maximum number of requests allowed within the window.

Window: The time period (in seconds) over which the limit is applied.

Active Time: The duration (in seconds) the tier remains active after being triggered.

Cooldown Time: The waiting period (in seconds) after the active time before the tier can be triggered again.
